# Beverage operations performance analytics

## Objective 
Analyse the production, downtime, waste, inventory, and order performance to indentify where operational losses occur and what actions can be taken to improve output and service levels. 

## Business context 
A beverage manufacturer is experiencing inconsistent line performance, variable levels of waste, and periodic service issues. Management requires a clear view of which lines, SKU's, and operational issues are responsible for performance loss. 

## Key questions
1. Which lines and SKU's contribute most to output?
2. Where is downtime concentrated?
3. Which products have the highest scrap rate?
4. Which SKU's create the greatest inventory risk?
5. How is operational performance affecting service levels?
6. What actions should management prioritise?

## Tools
- Excel: audit, checks, exploratory analysis.
- SQL: cleaning, transformation, and KPI views.
- Power BI: dashboarding and communication.

## Deliverables
- Excel analysis workbook.
- SQL script.
- Power BI dashboard.
- Case study with findings and business recommendations.
